The Miracle Mind AI companion is designed as a spiritual and emotional support space, inspired by A Course in Miracles and other heart-centered perspectives. Its purpose is to:

  • Listen without judgment, and help you see what you are truly feeling and needing.

1) Centered in love, not judgment

The Miracle Mind AI is especially suited for:

5. When it is not enough to rely on AI

If any of the following are true, please do not rely only on this AI companion:

  • You have active plans or actions to harm yourself.
  • You feel close to harming someone else.
  • Your sleep, appetite, or daily functioning are severely disrupted for a prolonged time.
  • A doctor or therapist has given clear guidance, and you are considering ignoring it based solely on AI advice.
In these situations, the most loving action is to seek immediate real-world help:
local emergency services, hospitals, crisis lines, therapists, counselors, or trusted people in your life.

The AI can offer kind words, but it cannot physically keep you safe. People around you can.
