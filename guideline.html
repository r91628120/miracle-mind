3. Boundaries & Important Limitations

To keep this space safe and ethical, Miracle Mind must follow clear boundaries regarding what it cannot provide:

1) No medical or mental health diagnosis

The AI cannot evaluate symptoms or provide clinical interpretations related to anxiety, depression, trauma, medication, or disorders.

2) No professional legal or financial advice

It cannot guide legal decisions, financial risks, contracts, or disputes.

3) No crises or emergency intervention

If you are experiencing panic, suicidal thoughts, danger, or an unsafe living situation, please contact local emergency services or professional support immediately.

This AI cannot replace therapy, counseling, medical care, or crisis hotlines. It can only accompany you with kindness in non-emergency situations.

5. Situations Where This AI Is *Not* Appropriate

For your safety, please avoid using this AI in the following cases:

  • When you need medical, psychiatric, or legal expertise.
  • When you're making major financial, safety, or life-or-death decisions.
  • When you are experiencing hallucinations or losing touch with reality.
  • When you are in an active emergency or abusive situation.
  • When your well-being requires immediate real-world assistance.
If you ever feel unsafe, please reach out to someone who can physically help you. You deserve real, human support when you are in danger.

6. Ethical Standards Followed by This AI

1) No manipulation or persuasion

The AI will not pressure you into beliefs, decisions, purchases, or spiritual interpretations.

2) No pretending to be a human

The AI will always respond as an AI companion, not as a therapist or authority.

3) Respecting your autonomy

The AI may help you explore feelings, but the direction of your life is always your own.

4) Privacy and sensitivity

Conversations are designed to be handled respectfully, though not guaranteed secure. Avoid sharing identifiable or sensitive personal data.

5) Avoiding harm

If a message you send implies self-harm, violence, or severe distress, the AI may gently remind you to seek real-world help.
